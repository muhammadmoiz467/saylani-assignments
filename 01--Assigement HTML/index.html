Mango
The mango fruit is roughly oval in shape, with uneven sides.The fruit is a drupe, with an outer flesh surrounding a stone. The flesh is soft and bright yellow-orange in color. The skin of the fruit is yellow-green to red.
Apple
The apple is a pome (fleshy) fruit, in which the ripened ovary and surrounding tissue both become fleshy and edible. ... When harvested, applea are usually roundish, 5-10 cm (2-4 inches) in diameter, and some shade of red, green, or yellow in colour, they vary in size, shape, and acidity depending on the variety.
