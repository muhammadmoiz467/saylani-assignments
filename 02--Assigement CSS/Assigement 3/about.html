OUR FOUNDATION

Bridging the Digital Divide Through Education

SMIT, an initiative of Saylani Welfare International Trust, is
dedicated to providing high-quality, modern IT training to
underprivileged and aspiring students across the globe. Our
mission is simple: to equip tbrhe next generation with the coding
and digital skills necessary to thrive in the 21st-century
economy, irrespective of their financial background.,We believe
access to top-tier technological education is a right, not a
privilege. By focusing intensely on practical application and
job readiness, we ensure our graduates are prepared to
compete in the global marketplace.
